How it works

It works through locally installed software that uses image recognition and AI enhanced interpretation to detect ads in any form that displayed on your screen. This goes beyond the basic advertising boxes on websites, it can even detect and target fake user posts on social media that are veiled advertisements.

What it does

After detecting an ad or otherwise harmful content on your screen, it actively scans over and replaces the content with a new graphic. Based on options that the user chose during setup, the graphic replacement can be anything from art to historic quotes, educational excerpts, comedic jokes,and much more.

Other Benefits

As a bonus we can not only detect and replace ads, but also detect Scams through emails or other websites that can help save the more vulnerable online users. This creates are far safer and more enjoyable internet browsing experience.
